Regular Service

  • We Diagnose

    We identify and analyze problems or issues with a vehicle's mechanical or electronic systems. Our Mechanics use diagnostic tools and techniques to pinpoint and understand vehicle issues for repair and maintenance while following professional guidelines.

  • We Repair

    At Juebs Nigeria Limited, we are specialists in the maintenance and repair of light, medium and heavy trucks. We can perform all mechanical servicing according to manufacturer's schedule, restoring their functionality and safety for continued use.

  • We Service

    We offer various mechanical servicing including steering and suspension, breaks, clutches, gear box and drive train. We can also replace and repair tyres, windscreen and other glass. We are specialists in servicing Mercedes, TATA and Toyota vehicles.

Routine Maintenance

This include Log book and scheduled servicing, safety inspections, Tyre and wheel alignment, Brake repairs/servicing, Clutch servicing and replacement etc

Transmission and Drivetrain

This involves the professional handling of both the Manual and automatic transmission repairs, and also the Gearbox maintenance and repairs

Glass and Windscreen Services

Windscreen and automotive glass repairs and maintenance involve fixing chips and cracks to ensure clear visibility, safety, and structural integrity of vehicle glass components.

Towing and Towbar Services

Towbar fitment and repairs entail professionally installing or fixing towbars on vehicles, ensuring secure and safe towing capabilities for various purposes, from trailers to caravans.

Fuel System Maintenance

Inspecting, cleaning, and repairing of fuel system components to optimize fuel delivery, ensuring efficient engine performance, fuel economy, and reduced emissions.

Specialized Vehicle Services

Our Heavy duty, medium, and light truck specialists excel in servicing and repairing diverse truck types, offering fleet maintenance solutions to enhance reliability, safety, and operational efficiency.
